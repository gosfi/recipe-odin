Sweet Tea

One of the nicest things to drink during a hot summer day

Ingredients

  • 3 small pouch of black tea
  • sugar
  • water

Steps

  1. boil some water
  2. once boiled put the water in a container made for putting liquid
  3. put your pouches in the water
  4. put as much sugar as you want, taste test to be sure it's not to bitter or too sweet
  5. stir with a wooden spoon
  6. remove the pouches from the container
  7. let it cool off in the fridge for a few hours (overnight if possible)
  8. Enjoy!
