Naan Pizza

You all know the classic italian dish named pizza
but we're making it with a slight twist, using naan as our dough.
That way you cut down on the time it takes to make a traditional pizza.
Perfect for hungry students and families with little ones.

Ingredients

  • some naan
  • pizza sauce
  • some grated cheese
  • meats of your choice
  • vegetables of your choice

Steps

  1. cut all of the vegetables
  2. preheat the oven at 350 degrees farenheit
  3. put your naans on a baking sheet
  4. put some sauce on each of your naans
  5. put your vegetables on your pizzas
  6. put your meats on the pizzas
  7. put your cheeses on the pizzas
  8. cook your pizzas for 20min
  9. Enjoy!
